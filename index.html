TALL DARK N' REFINED

Based in Vancouver, BC, Brewed Awakening has become one of the most talked-about online coffee stores in 2020. The journey started in May of 2019 where our founders met through a charity event, and mutually shared a love for coffee, as well as the act of giving. This unexpected group of strangers seemed like a match made in heaven, and soon ended up creating the Brewed Awakening project we know and love today. They hope to share their enthusiasm with their customers and inspire them to help contribute to helping others. Since the beginning, our store and community aim to represent our love, passion, and innovation to bring you quality blends and premium roasts.

OUR MISSION

We take pride in providing quality products for our beloved customers. All our beans are ethically and sustainably sourced and our packaging is 100% recyclable and biodegradable. Not only do we support our local suppliers, but small coffee bean farmers around the world contribute to creating the blends you love.

The same way you support our business by purchasing our products, we do what we can to support those in need. We commit to donating 15% of our sales profit to buy care packages for communities in need in Liberia, Africa. With you help, we can continue working towards our mission!
